Creating your own catalog of postcards
Spliting the collection into groups
More than 50 characteristics for a detailed postcard description
Filtering collections
Multiple-level sorting
Adding more than 10 images to the postcard
Unlimited adding links to files and web pages
Image viewer
Moving and copying postcard from collection to collection
Importing data from MS Excel files
Print reports with the ability export into MS Excel, MS Word and PDF
Working with external drive
