# MITCalc Torsion springs
The calculation is intended for the purposes of geometric and strength designs of spiral cylindrical torsion springs made of wires and rods in circular sections cold formed loaded with a static or cyclic loading Application is developed in MS Excel is multilanguage and supports Imperial and Metric units and solves the following main tasks
 Automatic design of the spring
 Selection of the optimal design of the spring with respect to strength geometry and weight
 Static and dynamic strength tests
 Calculation of the working forces of a spring with known production and mounting dimensions
 Calculation of mounting dimensions for known spring loading and production parameters
 The application includes a table of commonly used spring materials according to ISO ASTMSAE DIN BS JIS and others
 Support of D CAD AutoCAD AutoCAD LT IntelliCAD Ashlar Graphite TurboCAD and D CAD in full calculation package Autodesk Inventor SolidWorks
The calculation is based on data procedures and algorithms from specialized literature and the standards EN and DIN
