# Quick Timer R8X
Quick Timer R8X is high resolution programmable timer software to control up to 8-channel relay controller boards. Quick time setting and high resolution make it fit for lights control, science experiment, home automation, industry control, energy conservation and so on. It works with 8-channel relay controller boards of National Control Devices, Robot Electronics, and Velleman.
