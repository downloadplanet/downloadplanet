# MITCalc Tolerances
This calculation includes tables and calculations for easy option of fits of machine parts and determination of their dimensional tolerances and deviations Application is developed in MS Excel is multilanguage supports Imperial and Metric units and solves the following main tasks
 Selection of suitable fits of machine parts according to the international standard ISO 
 Determination of dimensional tolerances and deviations of machine parts according to the international standard ISO 
 Selection of preferred fits of machine parts and determination of their dimensional tolerances and deviations according to ANSI B
 Determination of nonprescribed limit deviations of linear and angular dimensions according to ISO 
 Automatic design of a fit for the given clearance or fit interference respectively
The data procedures algorithms and specialized literature and standards ANSI ISO DIN and others were used in the calculations
Used standards ANSI B ANSI B ISO ISO ISO EN JIS B
