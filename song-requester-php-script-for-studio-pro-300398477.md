# Song Requester PHP Script for Studio Pro<br />Requires StationPlaylist Studio Pro. Listeners may search for a song in your library and make a request from your website. The requested songs may be inserted automatically in predetermined positions in the playlist while automated, or may be inserted manually by a DJ doing a live show. Requests optionally include the name and location of the person requesting the song. Artist and song separation is taken into account. A voice track introduction may be inserted before every request to announce the following song is a request.
