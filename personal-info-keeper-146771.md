# Personal Info Keeper
Personal Info Keeper allow users to save their personal information, such as contact information, account numbers or anything that they frequently re-type, into a "drag and drop" facility. The application eliminates having to re-type the same information, over and over again. Personal Info Keeper allows you to quickly fill out forms on web pages that ask for information such as your account number, your name, address and telephone number and even your password.
