# Mac PDF2Text
Mac PDF2Text is a plugin conversion tool for Acrobat Used to convert your PDF documents into plain text files. Options are available to output page numbers plus text tolorance level for varing sizes of text. NOTE: requires full version of Acrobat 4,5 or 6 to operate correctly.
