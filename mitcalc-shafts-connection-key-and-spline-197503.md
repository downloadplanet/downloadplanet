# MITCalc Shafts connection key and spline
Geometric designs and strength checks of shaped connections of shafts with hubs Application is developed in MS Excel is multilanguage and supports Imperial and Metric units and solves the following main tasks
 Design of a connection with parallel side keys
 Design of a connection with Woodruffs keys
 Design of a connection with straightsided splines
 Design of a connection with involute splines
 Strength check of designed couplings
 The application includes a table of keys and splines according to ISO SAE DIN BS JIS and CSN
 Support of D CAD systems AutoCAD AutoCAD LT IntelliCAD Ashlar Graphite TurboCAD
Used standards ANSI B ANSI B ANSI B ANSI BM ISO R ISO ISO DIN DIN DIN DIN DIN DIN BS BS JIS B CSN CSN CSN CSN
