Meta-Morpher is a morphing program which transforms one picture step wise into another. For example one face into another face. The possiblities are various. You can convert animals in humans and converse. You can render as much steps between this two pictures as you want. You only have to choose the two pictures and select the areas that are similar and that should morph into each other. For example the head, the eyes and the mouth.
