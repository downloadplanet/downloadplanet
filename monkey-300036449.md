# Monkey
Using Monkey, you always have quick and easy access to your favorite folders, applications, documents, files and web adresses. You just move the mouse cursor to the upper screen edge so that Monkey will open up the Monkey Menu for you, that will ease your work a lot. Alternatively, you can access all those items using global hotkeys or even mouse gestures.
