# PDF Thumbnail Generator
Thumbnail for PDF document can be generated from a bitmap BMP or a JPEG JPG or even a EMF Encapsulated Meta File
When thumbnails are created and embedded into your PDF files you will see the thumbnails displayed on the left column of your PDF Reader or Viewer usually Adobe Reader
With this handy tool you can decide whether to show thumbnails when PDF file is opened
