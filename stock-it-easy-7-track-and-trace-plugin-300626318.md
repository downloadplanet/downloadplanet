# Stock It Easy 7 track and trace plugin
![Stock It Easy 7 track and trace plugin](https://mycommerce.akamaized.net/api/pimages/P300626318/BIG/300626318.JPG)
The extension "tracking and tracing" is the management of batches, serial numbers, limits date of use and all derivative uses of these dates. Upon receipt of your payment , you will receive an email containing the activation keys ordered. There are no additional download or install compared to Stock It Easy , simply open window management licenses in the menu / License on the main page. Above the "Submit" button you select from the list "Extension tracking and tracing", you enter your license key and click on the "Submit" button.
