# Photo Blend 3D
![Photo Blend 3D](https://mycommerce.akamaized.net/api/pimages/P300870541/BIG/300870541.JPG)
Photo Blend 3D is a new generation of photo montage software, designed from the ground up for the purpose of easy combining objects from several photographs into a single composition.
With its original features such as automatic color adjustment it will give you more time to focus on the creative and artistic aspect of the whole process. You can export the final composition as a normal 2D photo and you can very easy make a 3D stereography and anaglyph images as well.
