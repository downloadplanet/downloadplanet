# Shredder 13 Windows
![Shredder 13 Windows](https://mycommerce.akamaized.net/api/pimages/P300755604/BIG/300755604.PNG)
Shredder 13 Windows is a professional computer chess program by Stefan Meyer-Kahlen, the eighteen times world computer chess champion. Shredder 13 combines extremely powerful play with easy handling. The play of the program can be variably adjusted to any strength, so that everyone can find an adequate chess partner. Many functions for analysing one's own games as well as a built-in coach, who is alert of one's mistakes, help to improve your play. Shredder 13 is the latest and strongest version of the Shredder family, a series of the best and most successful chess programs in the last years.
