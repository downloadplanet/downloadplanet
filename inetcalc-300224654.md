# iNetCalc
Internet connection speed meter and bandwidth monitor with ad-banners and popups blocker. iNetCalc is used to visualize and control the speed of internet connections. It displays the current speed graphically and numerically. It also keeps records of transferred data amount, determines quality of your connections, shows how much money you have to pay for internet. Additionally, it kills ad-banners and annoying ad popup windows.
