# PDF-Fotoalbum
Now you can create you digital photo albums as professional PDF files! You can then print and distribute them, burn them on a CD, send them with email or publish them on internet! The advantage is that everyone can view your photoalbum exaclty as you wanted it with the Acrobat Reader and nobody needs to have a special software to display the album. You can give away or store your picture in a very compact way. You can type a free text for every picture which will display under the picture. You can freely deside how many picture should be placed on one page and which amount of space should be between the pictures. You can enter a caption and add page numbers too.
