# Dictaphone Registration
![Dictaphone Registration](https://mycommerce.akamaized.net/api/pimages/P300393674/BIG/300393674.PNG)
Dictaphone is a utility that lets you record audio Its optimized for recording dicatations but you can record anyhthing you like that your sound card supports
Other part of the application is the Recordings Manager that lets you manage the recordings

Features
Record dictations or anything else through the sound card
Continue recording from a specified position rewind and continue recording function
Record to WAV MP WMA or any command line encoder
Specify recording title and notes for a dictation
Automatically store recording date and time for the dictation
Manage you recordings

Unregistered version is a fully functional day trial version
